Eggs

Making eggs

Who doesn't love eggs! Such a simple recipe and such a good one too!

Eggs come out of chickens and stuff and they can be eaten if fried, unless one likes to eat raw eggs.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Butter
  1. Get eggs and put them on a frying pan
  2. Fry the eggs with oil or butter for a couple of minutes
  3. Preferably use a non stick pan or something
  4. Eat the eggs!
