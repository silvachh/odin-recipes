Toast

Get perfect toast every time

Who doesn't know how to make toast? Well, with this recipe, your toast will be the best ever!

Toast is bread sliced into thin pieces that is often grilled in a toaster or similar appliance to be crispier and warmer.

Ingredients

  • Toast
  • Butter
  • Blueberries
  • Syrup
  1. Get toast and put it in a toaster for two minutes
  2. Take toast and put it on a plate
  3. Take butter, or other Ingredients you have and put them on toast using a utensil or similar tool
  4. Eat the toast using your mouth and teeth
