Cornflakes

Cornflakes for breakfast

For most energy during the day, having breakfast is crucial. Therefore cornflakes is ideal for that because it is easy to make when you are groggy

Cornflakes is a dish usually eaten in the morning often with yoghurt or milk

Ingredients

  • Cornflakes
  • Milk
  • Yoghurt
  1. Get cornflakes and put it in a bowl
  2. Take milk or yoghurt and put it in a bowl in which you have set cornflakes in
  3. Eat the cornflakes
